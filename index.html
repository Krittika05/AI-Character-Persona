Welcome to Persona

Talk to 48 distinct AI characters across 6 themed universes.

1

Connect an AI (free option available)

Click the 🔑 icon and pick one:
FREE — Google Gemini: Get a key in 30 seconds at aistudio.google.com/app/apikey (no credit card). Generous free quota.
PREMIUM — Anthropic Claude: Get a key at console.anthropic.com (pay-as-you-go, very cheap).
Persona auto-detects which provider from the key.

2

Choose a theme

The dropdown next to the logo switches between Classic, Marvel, Anime, DC, Ghibli, and Disney — each with its own visual identity and 8 characters.

3

Pick a character

Click any card on the right. The profile panel shows their personality, specialty, voice, and what they're best for — plus example prompts you can click to start.

4

Chat in character

Each character responds in their own voice. Iron Man is sarcastic. Batman is terse. Naruto is loud. Switch characters or themes any time — history is saved per character.

Try these by theme

  • Marvel — Iron Man: "Help me debug this React component."
  • Anime — All Might: "I'm scared to give a big presentation tomorrow."
  • DC — Batman: "Walk me through a contingency plan for a launch."
  • Ghibli — Sophie: "How do I deal with a stubborn family member?"
  • Disney — Baymax: "I'm having a really hard day."
  • Classic — The Tech Sage: "Should I use Postgres or SQLite?"
